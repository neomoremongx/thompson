About Us

Thompson Electrical is a qualified and registered electrical contractor serving Bothaville and surrounding areas. We are registered with the Department of Labour and National Bargaining Council, ensuring all our work meets the highest standards of safety and quality.

With years of experience in the electrical industry, we specialize in both residential and commercial electrical services, from new installations to maintenance and repairs.

We ensure safe and quality work as your family and property safety is our priority. Our team of skilled electricians is committed to delivering exceptional service with attention to detail and customer satisfaction.
